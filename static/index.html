Cookies Manager

Use the "Get cookies.txt LOCALLY" browser extension to export cookies on a YouTube page. Upload here to download age-restricted or members-only videos.

How to Get Correct Cookies

Step 1: Install Extension

Search and install "Get cookies.txt LOCALLY" in the Chrome Web Store

Step 2: Log in to YouTube and Watch an Age-restricted Video

Go to youtube.com and log in — confirm your account avatar appears in the top-right

Then open any age-restricted video and confirm it plays normally (not showing "Sign in to confirm your age")

⚠ You must actually play an age-restricted video for Google to write the full auth token into cookies

Step 3: Export Cookies

On that YouTube page, click the extension icon and select "Export All Cookies" (not "Export")

⚠ Export on the page where you played the age-restricted video, don't switch to other sites first

Step 4: Upload

Upload the exported .txt file to this tool. It will automatically validate the required fields.

✅ Correct cookies should contain: __Secure-1PSID, __Secure-3PSID, SAPISID, LOGIN_INFO
